My Specialty

Description

A white rice, meat, and vegetable medley with white sauce and cheddar cheese mixed in. Enough food to last two people 3 meals a day for 3 days. The vegetables and meats included are optional, but add flavor.

Ingredients

  • White rice
  • Bacon
  • Sausage
  • Broccoli
  • Sliced baby bella mushrooms
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • White sauce
  • Cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Throw 2 and a half cups of white rice into a rice cooker.
  2. Add 2 and a half bottles of water into the rice cooker.
  3. Select the "white rice" option on the rice cooker, and then start.
  4. Pour 3 cups of frozen mixed vegetables into a pan and add a half cup of water.
  5. Cook at high heat for a minute, then lower to medium heat and add a lid.
  6. Stir every few minutes and cook vegetables for 7 minutes.
  7. Once done, set aside and pull out sausage.
  8. Thinly slice sausage, then put in a pan on medium heat.
  9. Add sliced baby bella mushrooms, turn to medium low heat, and add a lid to the pan.
  10. Stir every few minutes.
  11. While those simmer, slice broccoli and bring a pot of water to boil on high.
  12. Add sliced broccoli to the pot of water and boil for 4 minutes on medium heat.
  13. Slice bacon into thirds and add to medium low heat pan. Cook both sides till golden brown.
  14. Remove sausage and mushrooms from other pan once browned.
  15. Shred 3 cups of cheddar cheese.
  16. Add meat, vegetables, 1 cup of cheese, and rice to large glass baking dish and mix in the white sauce.
  17. Sprinkle cheddar cheese and parmesan on top of the dish.
  18. Set oven to broil high
  19. Put glass baking dish with ingredients in the oven for 10 minutes.
  20. Let cool 10 minutes and enjoy!
