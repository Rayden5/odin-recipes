Grilled Cheese

Description

Melted cheddar cheese on sourdough bread with bacon and avocado slices all come together to make the perfect grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Two slices sourdough bread
  • Bacon
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Slice bacon strips in half and cut avocado into slices.
  2. In one pan, cook bacon on medium low heat until golden brown.
  3. Slice cheese, but do not put on bread yet.
  4. In another pan, toast one side of bread on low heat until golden brown.
  5. Flip bread and put sliced cheese on the toasted side.
  6. Put lid over pan and cook until cheese melts.
  7. Combine cheesey bread, bacon, and acocado on a plate.
  8. Enjoy your meal!
